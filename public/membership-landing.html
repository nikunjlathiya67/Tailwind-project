We have two tiers of membership: Enthusiast and Equity. Both have access to services, events, and all clubhouses to meet the needs of your active lifestyle.

Enthusiast Membership

You may become an Enthusiast member as an individual, family/group or business. Each member pays an initiation fee and monthly dues.

True Community

Join our group of car enthusiasts and foster opportunity, as members often help one another grow personally and professionally.

Elevated Service

Drive enthusiast cars, take advantage of our Managed Car Services and witness operational excellence at inviting clubhouses.

Exclusive Events

Attend a variety of members-only events, including track days, poker nights, sporting events and destination drives.

Access the Collection

Rent from our enthusiast collection and drive your dream car(s) for special occasions or just the thrill of it.

View Collection

Managed Car Services

Our Managed Car Services are specifically designed for busy professionals. Leave behind the worries of car ownership and maintenance, and receive convenient solutions that align with your schedule.

Concierge

We honor all requests to preserve your car’s beauty, which can include mechanical and cosmetic services as well as transport.

Storage

We look after your car with the utmost care via a clean, secure, temperature-regulated indoor storage facility.

Detailing

To keep your car looking its best, we offer detailing services like Basic Wash, Deluxe Wash, Ultimate Detail and Ultimate Ceramic Detail.
